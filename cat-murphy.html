About Me

Cat is a second-year master's student and investigative journalism fellow at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland. She is a driven and confident reporter dedicated to using journalism to hold those in power accountable. She is a curious, eager reporter with coding experience and a gift for obtaining and analyzing data and public records.
